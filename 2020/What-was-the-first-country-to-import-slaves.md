It was… the first country.

In Sumerian cuneiform this is how you write “slave”

![](https://qph.fs.quoracdn.net/main-qimg-8c00ec195da088e70d7d7ee08587944d)

The three little marks inside the triangle are the word for “mountains”. The surrounding shape is the word for “male” If you’re a Sumerian from the flat plains of Mesopotamia, slave means “man from the mountains”. Or, as the internet puts it:
